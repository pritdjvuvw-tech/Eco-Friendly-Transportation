Tips for Green Mobility

1. Cycle or walk — for short trips, choose active transport. It’s zero‑emission and healthy.
2. Share rides — carpooling with colleagues or friends cuts emissions per person dramatically.
3. Go electric — if you drive, consider an EV or plug‑in hybrid. Charge with green energy.
4. Use public transport — buses, trams, and trains are far more efficient per passenger.
5. Plan efficient routes — combine errands, avoid traffic, and reduce unnecessary mileage.

Benefits of Green Transport

Cleaner air — reduced NOx and particulate matter improve respiratory health.
Less noise — electric and non‑motorized transport creates quieter cities.
Cost savings — less fuel, maintenance, and parking expenses.
Efficiency — dedicated lanes and shared mobility reduce travel time.
Climate action — lower greenhouse gas emissions help combat global warming.
